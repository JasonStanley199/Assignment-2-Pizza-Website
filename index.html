History of BRUNO

The founder and current owner, Mr. Bruno, started its business in 2010.

His business grew over the year, but started to escalate significantly in 2016 when USA'S former President, Mr. Barrack Obama, visited and enjoyed Bruno's Pizza.

Since then, customers began showing up and enjoyed Bruno's Pizza to the point where Mr. Bruno could open several branches throughout the state of Victoria.
